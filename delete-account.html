Delete Your AnchorCheck Account

You can request account deletion through AnchorCheck or by emailing harborpeaklabs@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request."

What Deletion Does

  • Deletes your AnchorCheck account and synchronized cloud financial data within 30 days of verified request
  • Removes your data from our servers, subject to limited legal retention requirements (fraud prevention, tax, legal compliance)

What Deletion Does Not Do

  • Cancel a Google Play subscription — you must cancel through Google Play separately
  • Remove exported backups you have saved — those remain wherever you saved them and must be deleted by you
  • Affect data on devices that haven't synced before the request completes

Before You Confirm

Before we confirm deletion, you'll be asked whether to also remove local data from the requesting device.

Important: Deleting your account does not cancel your Google Play subscription. To stop future charges, cancel your subscription in Google Play ₒ Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions before or after requesting account deletion.

Request by Email

Send "Account Deletion Request" to harborpeaklabs@gmail.com from the email address associated with your Google account. We will verify your identity before processing.

Response time: within 30 days of verified request.
