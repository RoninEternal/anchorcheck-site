Frequently Asked Questions

What does AnchorCheck Free include? Free supports up to 5 obligations (bills, subscriptions, debts, insurance) and 1 income source. You can view dashboard summary cards but not interact with Debt Portfolio, Savings Portfolio, or Cash Flow drill-down. Backup and restore are available in Free.
What does Pro add? Pro unlocks unlimited obligations and income sources, full dashboard interactions, the complete Debt Planner, and the Savings Portfolio.
How does the trial work? A 14-day free trial is available once per AnchorCheck account. You select a plan (monthly or annual) when starting the trial. No charge until the trial ends.
How do I manage or cancel my subscription? Through Google Play → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions. Deleting the AnchorCheck app does not cancel a subscription.
How do I back up my data? Use the Export option in AnchorCheck settings. Store the backup somewhere secure — it contains your financial planning information.
What if my restore fails? Do not reset or uninstall the app. Contact harborpeaklabs@gmail.com with your AnchorCheck version, device model, Android version, and the exact error message shown.
I switched phones. How do I get my data back? Android may restore app data through its automatic backup if your device and account are eligible. For a manual restore, use an exported backup.
How do I delete my AnchorCheck account? Visit the Account Deletion page or use the Account section inside AnchorCheck.
Can I use AnchorCheck on Windows? A Windows release is not currently available. Check back for future announcements.
