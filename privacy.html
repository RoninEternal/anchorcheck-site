Privacy Policy

Published by: Harbor Peak Labs  |  Contact: harborpeaklabs@gmail.com  |  Effective date: 2026-06-18

This policy covers the Android release of AnchorCheck. A Windows release is not currently available.

1. What AnchorCheck Is

AnchorCheck is a personal financial planning tool that helps you organize bills, debts, subscriptions, savings goals, and paycheck schedules. It is not a bank, accountant, tax service, credit counselor, or financial adviser.

2. Information You Provide

When you use AnchorCheck, you may enter:

  • Bill names, due dates, amounts, and categories
  • Subscription names and renewal dates
  • Debt names, balances, interest rates, and payment history
  • Income source names, pay schedules, and expected amounts
  • Savings goal names, balances, targets, and contributions
  • Notes, preferences, and planning settings

This information is stored locally on your device.

Do not enter bank account numbers, card numbers, Social Security numbers, passwords, or credentials. AnchorCheck is not designed to hold them and does not need them.

3. Account Information

AnchorCheck requires a Google account at sign-in. We receive a stable account identifier and limited Google profile information (such as name and email address) needed for authentication. We do not receive or store your Google password.

4. Purchases

Android purchases use Google Play Billing. We process purchase status, product identifier, entitlement type, purchase token, and renewal or expiration status. Payment card details are handled by Google Play, not by Harbor Peak Labs.

5. Device Storage and Backup

Android application state is stored in private app storage. Android Auto Backup is configured to include only the application's canonical preferences file. Backup availability and timing are controlled by Android and the backup account on your device. You may export a portable JSON backup at any time.

6. Analytics and Crash Reporting

AnchorCheck uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand how the app is used and to diagnose failures. These services do not receive bill names, debt names, balances, amounts, notes, or exported financial records.

7. How We Use Information

Information may be used to:

  • Save, restore, and display your financial plan
  • Authenticate your account
  • Verify purchases and provide entitled features
  • Provide customer support
  • Diagnose crashes and improve reliability
  • Measure app usage without including financial content
  • Meet legal and store-policy obligations

Harbor Peak Labs does not sell user-entered financial planning data.

8. When Information May Be Disclosed

Information may be disclosed:

  • To service providers needed to operate identity, billing, analytics, crash reporting, and support (including Google)
  • When you direct an export or share action
  • When required by valid legal process
  • To protect users, Harbor Peak Labs, or others from fraud, abuse, or harm
  • As part of a business transaction, subject to applicable notice and law

9. Account Deletion

You may delete your AnchorCheck account through the app or at the Account Deletion page. After identity verification, your account and synchronized cloud data will be deleted within 30 days, subject to limited legal retention requirements. Exported backups remain under your control and must be deleted by you. Account deletion does not automatically cancel a Google Play subscription.

10. Security

Harbor Peak Labs uses reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. No storage or transmission method is completely secure.

11. Children

AnchorCheck is not intended for children under 13.

12. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or information about how we process your data. Send requests to harborpeaklabs@gmail.com.

13. Changes

We may update this policy as the product changes. We will update the effective date and communicate material changes through a reasonable method.
